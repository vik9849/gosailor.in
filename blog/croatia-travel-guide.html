Croatia Travel Guide 2026: Game of Thrones Meets Adriatic Paradise

📅 April 2026⏱ 7 min read✍ GoSailor Travel Team

Croatia has shot up global search rankings in 2026, driven by its fame as the filming location of Game of Thrones and a growing reputation as one of Europe's most beautiful destinations. The Adriatic coastline, with its 1,200 islands, medieval walled cities, crystal-clear waters and excellent cuisine, offers a travel experience that combines natural beauty, history and adventure in perfect proportions.

💡 Visa: Croatia joined the Schengen Zone in 2023. EU citizens need just ID/passport. Non-EU visitors from visa-exempt countries (US, UK, Australia) need ETIAS from 2026 — apply at etias.com for €7.

Top Destinations in Croatia

  • 🏰 Dubrovnik — Walk the city walls, Game of Thrones filming location, stunning
  • 🏛 Split — Live inside a Roman emperor's palace — Diocletian's Palace
  • 🌊 Hvar Island — Most glamorous island, lavender fields, crystal water
  • 💧 Plitvice Lakes National Park — Turquoise lakes and waterfalls, UNESCO site
  • 🎭 Korčula — Rumored birthplace of Marco Polo, beautiful medieval town

Croatia by Yacht — The Ultimate Experience

Croatia is one of the world's top sailing destinations — 1,200 islands with hidden coves, secluded beaches and charming harbor towns that are only accessible by boat. A skippered yacht charter is the best way to experience the Dalmatian coast.
