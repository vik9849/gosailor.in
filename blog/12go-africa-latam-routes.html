Egypt

Egypt Is Having Its Biggest Travel Moment in Years

I'll be honest — when Egypt started climbing the 12Go booking charts, my first reaction was finally. The country has always deserved more love from international travelers. But something specific has changed: people are no longer treating Egypt as a single-pyramid, single-city trip. They're doing proper multi-city circuits, and the route data reflects it perfectly.

🇪🇬 Egypt — Popular 12Go Routes

What I love about these four routes is how well they tell a story together. The Luxor–Giza and reverse booking suggests travelers are doing a full Nile Valley sweep — ancient temples in the south, pyramids in the north. The Hurghada–Giza route tells you something specific about who's booking: Red Sea beach lovers tacking on a pyramid visit. Smart tourism. The Luxor–Aswan leg is the classic overnight sleeper train route, one of the most atmospheric journeys in Africa.

🏛 Travel Tip Egypt routes shine brightest for multi-city cultural itineraries. A 10-day Egypt circuit (Giza → Luxor → Aswan + Red Sea detour) is a complete trip in itself. Colombia

Colombia: Santa Marta to Cartagena Is the Caribbean Route Everyone Wants

If you've been to Colombia, you already know. If you haven't, consider this your sign. The Santa Marta to Cartagena de Indias run is one of those journeys that feels like a greatest-hits album of a country — jungle mountains rolling into pastel colonial plazas rolling into open Caribbean sea.

Cartagena's walled city — jacaranda-draped balconies, centuries-old cobblestones, and some of the best street food in South America.

🇨🇴 Colombia — Popular 12Go Route
Santa Marta → Cartagena de Indias Book on GoSailor

The traveler profile for this route is fascinating. People booking Santa Marta are usually mid-trip — they've done Bogotá or Medellín, maybe hiked to the legendary Ciudad Perdida, and now they want the coast. The Santa Marta to Cartagena leg is how they bridge that. Transport options include comfortable intercity buses, shared taxis, and short flights — 12Go aggregates all of them so you can compare properly.

On the Cartagena side, use GoSailor.in's hotel search — powered by Agoda's 2 million+ properties — to find everything from boutique guesthouses inside the walled city to beachfront resorts on Bocagrande. The price range is wide; the quality is high.

🌴 GoSailor Tip Colombia's Caribbean coast has less content competition than Mexico or Peru — which means now is a great time to explore it. Tanzania

Tanzania: The Island-to-Mainland Route That Quietly Blew Up

Here's the one that genuinely surprised me. Zanzibar to Dar es Salaam doesn't sound flashy on paper, but the 12Go booking numbers don't lie. More travelers are pairing a Zanzibar beach holiday with time on the Tanzanian mainland — and Dar es Salaam is finally getting the recognition it deserves.

Zanzibar's north coast — the kind of white-sand, turquoise-water beach that keeps people in East Africa longer than they planned.

🇹🇿 Tanzania — Popular 12Go Route
Zanzibar → Dar es Salaam Book on GoSailor

The ferry between these two is genuinely an experience in itself — less than two hours across the Indian Ocean, sometimes with dolphins spotted alongside. You arrive into one of East Africa's most lively port cities, with incredible Swahili food, a buzzing harbour, and easy access to safari gateways heading west and north.

The 12Go booking flow covers both the fast and slow ferry options, giving travelers real flexibility. If you're looking to extend into mainland Tanzania, GoSailor.in's car rental search makes it easy to hire a vehicle in Dar for the drive to Mikumi or Selous game reserves.

🌊 Smart Booking Tip Don't just book the ferry. The Zanzibar–Dar combination is best treated as two experiences: 4–5 days on the island (book your Zanzibar hotel through GoSailor.in to compare Agoda's live prices), then a ferry crossing to Dar for 2–3 days of city exploration before flying home.
