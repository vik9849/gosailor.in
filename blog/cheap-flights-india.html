Best Time of Day to Search and Book

Airlines typically release new fares and sales at specific times. The best windows to search for cheap flights are:

  • Tuesday 1am–3am — Airlines release weekend sales at midnight, prices drop
  • Wednesday morning — Competing airlines match Tuesday's deals
  • Early morning any day (5am–7am) — Least competition from other buyers, algorithms haven't caught up yet

Always search in incognito/private mode. Flight booking sites track your searches and raise prices when you return to the same search. Private mode removes this tracking.

9 Proven Hacks to Get the Cheapest Flights

  • ✅ Use incognito mode — stops price tracking by booking sites
  • ✅ Search nearby airports — sometimes a 30-minute drive saves £150
  • ✅ Be flexible by 1–2 days — shifting departure by 48 hours often saves 20%
  • ✅ Book connecting flights separately — sometimes cheaper than direct
  • ✅ Use GoSailor to compare — we search 100+ airlines simultaneously
  • ✅ Set price alerts — get notified when prices drop on your route
  • ✅ Check budget airline websites directly — Ryanair, easyJet, IndiGo don't always appear on aggregators
  • ✅ Fly into secondary airports — Stansted vs Heathrow, Bergamo vs Milan
  • ✅ Book early morning or late night flights — 20–30% cheaper than midday
