Types of Yacht Charter

  • Bareboat charter — You sail the yacht yourself (need sailing licence)
  • Skippered charter — Professional captain included, most popular option
  • Crewed charter — Full crew including chef, stewards — ultimate luxury
  • Catamaran charter — Stable and spacious, perfect for families

How Much Does a Yacht Charter Cost?

  • Small sailboat (2-4 people): from €500–€1,500 per week
  • Medium yacht (6-8 people): from €1,500–€5,000 per week
  • Large catamaran (8-12 people): from €3,000–€10,000 per week
  • Luxury motor yacht: from €10,000+ per week
