Vietnam Travel Guide 2026: The Perfect Budget Adventure

📅 April 2026⏱ 7 min read✍ GoSailor Travel Team

Vietnam is consistently one of the top trending travel destinations worldwide — and in 2026 it sits firmly in the global top 15 most-searched destinations. With jaw-dropping natural scenery, ancient towns, incredible street food, and some of the lowest prices of any destination in Southeast Asia, Vietnam delivers extraordinary value for travelers from every country.

💡 Visa: Vietnam offers e-visa for most nationalities — apply at evisa.xuatnhapcanh.gov.vn. Costs $25, approved within 3 business days. Valid for 90 days. Many countries also get visa-free entry — check before applying.
