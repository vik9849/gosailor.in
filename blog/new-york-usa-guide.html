New York City Travel Guide 2026: The City That Never Sleeps

📅 April 2026⏱ 7 min read✍ GoSailor Travel Team

New York City is one of the most visited and most searched cities in the world. The Big Apple delivers an unmatched urban experience — from the bright lights of Times Square to the serenity of Central Park, world-class museums, incredible food from every culture, and a raw energy that no other city on earth can match.

💡 Visa info: Most visitors need either a US visa or ESTA (Electronic System for Travel Authorization) for visa-exempt countries. ESTA costs $21 and is valid for 2 years. Apply at esta.cbp.dhs.gov

Free Things to Do in New York

  • 🗽 Staten Island Ferry — Best view of Statue of Liberty, completely FREE
  • 🌿 Central Park — 843 acres of urban paradise, free always
  • 🌉 Brooklyn Bridge Walk — Iconic walk with stunning views, free
  • 🎨 The High Line — Elevated park with art, free entry
  • 🏛 Metropolitan Museum of Art — Pay what you wish for non-NY residents
  • 🛍 Fifth Avenue shopping — Window shopping is free!

Top Paid Attractions

  • 🌃 Empire State Building — Observatory deck from $44
  • 🔭 Top of the Rock (Rockefeller) — From $40, best NYC views
  • 🗽 Statue of Liberty & Ellis Island — Ferry and entry from $24
  • 🎭 Broadway Show — From $50 for rush tickets, up to $300+ for premium
