Spain & Barcelona Travel Guide 2026: Sun, Art & Fiesta

📅 April 2026⏱ 7 min read✍ GoSailor Travel Team

Spain ranks #6 globally for most searched travel destinations in 2026. Barcelona alone draws millions of visitors each year with its extraordinary architecture, world-class food scene, beautiful beaches and vibrant culture. Combine this with Madrid, Seville, Granada and the stunning Spanish countryside and you have one of the world's most complete travel experiences.

💡 ETIAS 2026: Non-EU visitors from visa-exempt countries need ETIAS authorization before entering Spain. Apply at etias.com — costs €7. EU citizens just need their passport or ID.

Must-See in Barcelona

  • ⛪ Sagrada Família — Gaudí's masterpiece — book months ahead — from €26
  • 🌿 Park Güell — Magical Gaudí park — timed entry €10
  • 🏖 Barceloneta Beach — City beach, completely free
  • 🛍 Las Ramblas — Famous boulevard, free to walk
  • 🎨 Picasso Museum — World's best Picasso collection — €14
  • 🏙 Gothic Quarter — Medieval streets, free to explore

Barcelona Food Guide

  • Tapas at a local bar: €2–4 per tapa
  • Menu del dia (set lunch): €10–15 for 3 courses with wine
  • La Boqueria Market: fresh food from €1
  • Churros with chocolate: €3
