Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

GoSailor.in is a flight meta-search engine operated from Hyderabad, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide: If you create an account or sign up for price alerts, we collect your name and email address.

Search data: We collect information about the searches you perform — origin, destination, dates, and number of passengers — to show you relevant results and improve our service.

Usage data: We automatically collect information about how you use our site, including pages visited, time spent, and clicks, through cookies and analytics tools.

Device data: We collect basic device and browser information such as IP address, browser type, and operating system for security and analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our flight search service
  • To send price alerts and notifications you have subscribed to
  • To analyse usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Partners

When you click through to an airline or travel agency from GoSailor, you leave our platform. Those third-party sites have their own privacy policies, which govern how they handle your data. We recommend you review their policies before making a booking.

We use the following third-party services on our platform:

  • Travelpayouts — flight search widget and affiliate tracking
  • Google Analytics — usage analytics
  • Cloudflare — DNS and security

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience. See our Cookie Policy for full details on what cookies we use and how to control them.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Search data and analytics are retained for up to 24 months. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@gosailor.in.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also withdraw consent for marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email we send. To exercise your rights, contact us at hello@gosailor.in.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests: hello@gosailor.in
