Booking flights with GoSailor
How does GoSailor work? +
GoSailor searches hundreds of airlines and travel sites simultaneously to find you the cheapest flights. We compare prices in real time so you always get the best deal available.
How can I find the cheapest flight? +
Enter your origin, destination and travel dates, then click Search. GoSailor will show you all available flights sorted by price. Try flexible dates for even cheaper options.
Do I book my flight with GoSailor? +
GoSailor shows you the best prices from trusted travel sites. When you find a flight you like, we direct you to the airline or travel site to complete your booking safely.
What happens after I book my flight? +
Once you complete your booking on the airline or travel site, you will receive a confirmation email directly from them. GoSailor does not handle bookings or payments.
Can I book a flexible flight ticket? +
Yes! Many airlines now offer flexible tickets. Look for the "Flexible dates" option in our search results to compare prices across different travel dates.
What is a Price Alert? +
Sign up and log in to set price alerts for your favourite routes. We will email you when prices drop so you never miss a great deal.
