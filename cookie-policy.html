What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and show you relevant content.

Third-Party Cookies

Our booking partners (airlines and travel agencies) may also set cookies on your device when you click through from GoSailor. These are governed by their own cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of GoSailor and other websites.

Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
