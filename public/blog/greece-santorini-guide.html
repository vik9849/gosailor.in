Greece & Santorini Travel Guide 2026: Complete Island Hopping Guide

📅 April 2026⏱ 7 min read✍ GoSailor Travel Team

Greece ranks #12 on the world's most searched destinations for 2026 — and Santorini alone drives millions of searches every month. With its iconic white-washed buildings, blue-domed churches, incredible sunsets and world-class food, Greece delivers a travel experience unlike any other. Add Athens, Mykonos, Crete and Rhodes and you have endless island-hopping possibilities.

💡 ETIAS 2026: Non-EU visitors from visa-exempt countries need ETIAS authorization. Apply at etias.com — €7. EU citizens just need their passport.

Best Greek Islands to Visit

  • 🌅 Santorini — Most iconic sunsets, best for romance and photography
  • 🎉 Mykonos — World-class nightlife, beautiful beaches
  • 🏖 Crete — Largest island, incredible beaches and history
  • 🌿 Corfu — Lush green island, Venetian architecture
  • ⛵ Rhodes — Medieval old town, crystal-clear waters
  • 🧘 Paros — Less crowded, authentic Greek village life

Must-Do in Athens

  • 🏛 Acropolis — Most important ancient site in the world, entry €20
  • 🏺 Acropolis Museum — World-class museum, entry €10
  • 🍽 Monastiraki Flea Market — Street food, souvenirs, free
