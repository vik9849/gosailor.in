Istanbul & Turkey Travel Guide 2026: Where East Meets West

📅 April 2026⏱ 7 min read✍ GoSailor Travel Team

Istanbul is one of the most magnificent cities on earth — a place where Europe meets Asia, where Byzantine churches stand next to Ottoman mosques, and where the call to prayer echoes across ancient rooftops. Turkey as a whole offers extraordinary value for travelers in 2026, with incredible history, world-class cuisine, stunning landscapes and some of the best prices in the Mediterranean region.

💡 Visa info: Many nationalities including US, UK, EU can get Turkish e-Visa online at evisa.gov.tr. Costs approximately $50–100 depending on nationality. Approved within minutes.

Must-See in Istanbul

  • 🕌 Hagia Sophia — 1,500-year-old wonder, entry free (mosque)
  • 🕌 Blue Mosque — 6 minarets, stunning tiles, free entry
  • 🏛 Topkapi Palace — Ottoman sultans' palace, entry from ₺800
  • 🛍 Grand Bazaar — 4,000+ shops, free to browse
  • 🌊 Bosphorus Cruise — Europe to Asia by boat, from ₺100

Beyond Istanbul — Turkey's Best

  • 🎈 Cappadocia — Hot air balloon rides over fairy chimneys — unmissable
  • 🏖 Bodrum/Antalya — World-class beach resorts on the Mediterranean
  • 🌊 Pamukkale — Natural white travertine terraces and thermal pools
