Bali Travel Guide 2026: The Island of the Gods

📅 April 2026⏱ 7 min read✍ GoSailor Travel Team

Bali is one of the most searched travel destinations worldwide in 2026 — and it's easy to see why. This Indonesian island offers an extraordinary combination of ancient Hindu culture, lush tropical landscapes, world-class surf beaches, spiritual retreats and vibrant nightlife, all at prices that suit every budget.

💡 Visa: Most nationalities get visa on arrival in Bali for 30 days. Cost approximately $35. Extendable for another 30 days. US, UK, EU, Australian citizens all eligible.

Top Things to Do in Bali

  • 🌾 Tegallalang Rice Terraces — UNESCO-listed, stunning Instagram shots, free to walk
  • 🛕 Tanah Lot Sea Temple — Iconic sunset temple, entry IDR 60,000
  • 🐒 Sacred Monkey Forest Ubud — 700+ macaques, entry IDR 80,000
  • 🌊 Kuta Beach — World famous surf, free entry
  • 🌋 Mount Batur Sunrise Trek — Active volcano hike, from $35 with guide
  • 💆 Traditional Balinese Spa — Full body massage from $10
  • 🎨 Ubud Art Market — Handmade crafts and textiles, great for souvenirs

Best Time to Visit Bali

  • April–October: Dry season — best weather for beaches and trekking
  • July–August: Peak season — most expensive, book early
  • November–March: Rainy season — 40% cheaper, lush green landscapes
