Paris Travel Guide 2026: The City of Light Awaits

📅 April 2026⏱ 7 min read✍ GoSailor Travel Team

Paris is consistently among the world's top travel destinations — and in 2026, France sits at #4 globally for most searched travel destinations. The City of Light delivers romance, world-class cuisine, incredible art and architecture, and that ineffable Parisian atmosphere that keeps travelers coming back again and again.

Must-See Attractions in Paris

  • 🗼 Eiffel Tower — Book tickets online in advance — summit from €29
  • 🖼 The Louvre — World's largest art museum — entry €17, free first Sunday
  • ⛪ Notre-Dame Cathedral — Reopened in 2024 after restoration — free entry
  • 🎨 Musée d'Orsay — Impressionist masterpieces — entry €16
  • 🏰 Palace of Versailles — Day trip from Paris — entry €21
  • 🛍 Champs-Élysées — World's most famous avenue — free to walk

Paris on a Budget

  • Free museums on first Sunday of each month
  • Eat at bakeries and markets — croissant from €1.20
  • Use Metro day pass — €14 for unlimited travel
  • Stay in arrondissements 10–20 for cheaper hotels
  • Paris Museum Pass covers 50+ museums — €52 for 2 days
