Booking issues?

GoSailor is a search engine — we don't process bookings directly. For issues with a booking, please contact the platform where you booked:

  • Flights — contact the airline directly or the travel agency you booked through
  • Hotels — contact Agoda support at agoda.com/help
  • Car rentals — contact LocalRent at localrent.com
  • Activities — contact GetYourGuide at getyourguide.com/help
  • Flight compensation — use AirHelp at airhelp.com
