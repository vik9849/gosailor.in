About GoSailor

India's travel search engine — built in Hyderabad

What is GoSailor?

GoSailor.in is a travel search engine based in Hyderabad, India. We help Indian travellers find the cheapest flights, best hotels, car rentals, tours and cruise charters worldwide — all in one place.

We search hundreds of airlines and thousands of hotel partners simultaneously so you don't have to. Compare prices, browse results, and book with confidence on our trusted partner sites.

Our Mission

Travel should be accessible to everyone. We built GoSailor to make it easier for Indian travellers to find the best deals without visiting 10 different websites.

We're a meta-search engine — we never mark up prices or add hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay.

How We Make Money

GoSailor earns a small commission from our booking partners (airlines, hotels, Agoda, GetYourGuide, LocalRent) when you complete a booking after clicking from our site. This never affects the price you pay — it comes from the partner's side.

We are completely ad-free. We never sell your data to advertisers.
