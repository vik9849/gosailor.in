About GoSailor.in

India's flight meta-search engine — we find deals, you book direct

What is GoSailor?

GoSailor.in is a flight meta-search engine based in Hyderabad, India. We search hundreds of airlines and online travel agencies simultaneously to show you the best available flight prices — all in one place.

⚠️ Important: GoSailor does not sell flights, process payments, or issue tickets. We are a search and comparison tool only. All bookings are completed directly with the airline or travel agency on their own website.

Think of us like a price comparison site — we show you what's available and where to get the best deal, then we direct you there to complete your purchase safely.

How GoSailor Works

1. Search: Enter your origin, destination, and travel dates. GoSailor searches live prices from airlines and travel agencies in real time.

2. Compare: View all available flights sorted by price, duration, or number of stops. Filter by baggage, layovers, departure time, and more.

3. Book: When you find a flight you like, click through to the airline or travel agency's website. Your booking, payment, and ticket confirmation happen entirely on their platform — not on GoSailor.

4. Fly: Your booking confirmation comes directly from the airline or agency. GoSailor has no involvement in your booking after the redirect.

Our Role & Limitations

As a meta-search engine, GoSailor has no control over:

— Flight prices (set by airlines and agencies)
— Seat availability (managed by airlines)
— Booking terms, cancellations, or refunds (handled by your booking provider)
— Baggage policies (set by individual airlines)
— Customer service for bookings (contact your airline or agency directly)

If you have a problem with a booking — cancellation, refund, missed flight, lost baggage — please contact the airline or travel agency where you booked directly. GoSailor cannot access or modify your reservation.

How We Make Money

GoSailor earns a small commission when users click through to our partner airlines and travel agencies and complete a booking. This does not affect the price you pay — you always pay the same price you would by going directly to the airline or agency.

We are committed to showing you accurate, unbiased results. Our search rankings are based on price and relevance, not commission rates.
